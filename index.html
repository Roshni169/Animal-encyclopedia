Animal encyclopedia


Mammals are warm blooded Animals that genarally give birth to live young.

fish are underwater animals that breathe through gills. There are over 30000 known species of fish.

Birds are a group of warm blooded animals. Most have beaks and feathers, that help them fly.

Reptiles are mostly cold blooded animals, that are covered in scales and have a bony external plate (such as a shell).

Amphibians are a group of cold blooded animals that also have gills to breathe underwater. Furthermore, most Amphibians lay eggs without shells.
